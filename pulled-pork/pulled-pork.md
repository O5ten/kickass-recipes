#Richards Pulled Pork

##Requirements
- 2 kilograms boneless tenderloin pork
- 2 stora gula lökar
##Spices 
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon kanel
- salt n pepper
- chilipowder
- paptrikapowder
Mix the spices above

##Preparing
- Cut the onions in large onion rings
- Put in the bottom of the pot
- Cook as much meatbullion that it covers the onion
- Put some stubs barbeque sauce and worchestersauce in it
- OPTIONAL: Some garlic

##Slow Cooking
Put it in a oven secure form and place 
it in 70-80*c for 10-12 hours for best effect. 

##When done
It will be alot of fluids so rinse it out so you get all the onions out. 
Take 2 forks and shred the meat. 

Serve with other dish och freeze to use later.
